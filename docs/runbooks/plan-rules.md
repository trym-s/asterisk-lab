# Plan Rules

- Keep exactly one root `PLANS.md`.
- Keep it short and scannable, generally 100 to 250 lines.
- Always include: Status, Governing spec, Active milestones, Blockers,
  Canonical evidence, Recent updates, and Archive pointers.
- Update it after meaningful work when status, blockers, evidence, or next
  actions change.
- Link evidence instead of pasting long logs.
- Archive completed plans under `docs/archive/plan/` with a dated topic name.
